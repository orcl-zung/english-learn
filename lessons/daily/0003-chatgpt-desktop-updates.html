ChatGPT desktop app — updates

Evening! We've gotten lots of great feedback on the new ChatGPT desktop app (which we didn't get totally quite right on the first try), and as a result, we've made some changes.

1/ ChatGPT conversation history and projects are now visible in the sidebar. Also, your Chat and Work history now sync across web, mobile, and desktop. Local tasks still stay on your computer.
2/ You can now easily switch between Chat and Work modes inside ChatGPT on desktop, which is now also consistent with how it shows on web and mobile.
3/ Nothing is changing for users on Codex mode. It's still the OG and best at what it does.

And overall we're continuing to fix paper cuts and improve performance, reliability, and efficiency.

Keep up the feedback, hope you like the updates! Seen on X (Twitter) — a product / release update. TTS audio below is read from a cleaned version (the 1/2/3 markers removed) so it sounds natural.

连读地图 （五个特征）

C → V
bes·tat wha·tit does/best æt wɒt ɪt/ → /bes·tæt wɒ·tɪt/
best 末尾的 /t/ 滑到 at；what 的 /t/ 滑到 it——一句里塞了两个 C→V。
C → C · 长读
keep pup/kiːp ʌp/ → /kiː:pʌp/
两个 /p/ 黏成一个长 /p/（gemination），不分两次。（第二课）
C → C · 吞音
didn't get/dɪdnt ɡet/ → /dɪdn ɡet/
didn't 的 /t/ 夹在 /n/ 和 /ɡ/ 中间 → 被吞掉。
V → V · /j/
OG·j·and best/əʊ dʒiː ænd/ → /əʊ dʒiː·j·ænd/
字母 G 以 /iː/ 收尾，接 and 的元音 → 塞一个轻轻的 /j/。
V → V · /w/
how·w·it shows/haʊ ɪt/ → /haʊ·w·ɪt/
how 的 /aʊ/ 圆唇收尾，滑出一个 /w/ 把两个元音粘起来。
弱读
lots·ə great/lɒts əv/ → /lɒts ə/
虚词 of 弱读到只剩 /ə/，几乎听不见。

短语 & 搭配 （这条藏着美式陷阱，注意）

gotten vs got 美式 · 英音用 got
"We've gotten lots of feedback" 是美式。英音里过去分词是 got：We've got lots of feedback。你练的是英音，写作/口语请用 got。读美媒时认识 gotten 即可。
as a result 通用 · 正式
"因此 / 结果"。连接因果的利器，雅思写作衔接（cohesion）高分用法。常放句首：..., and as a result, we've made changes.
get [sth] right / on the first try 通用
"didn't get it quite right on the first try" = 第一次没完全做对。搭配：get it right（做对）、on the first/second try（第一次/第二次尝试）。道歉+承认问题，很地道。
sync across [devices] 科技
history now sync across web, mobile, and desktop。sync = synchronize 缩写，"跨…同步"。搭配：sync across / sync with / stay in sync。
consistent with 通用 · 正式
"与…保持一致"。consistent with how it shows on web。雅思好用：The results are consistent with previous findings.
paper cuts 地道习语
字面"纸割伤"，引申为一堆小烦人的毛病——不严重但硌人。fix paper cuts / smooth out paper cuts。产品/管理圈常用，比 "minor bugs" 形象得多。
the OG 俚语 · 慎写入
= Original Gangster 的缩写，现泛指"原版 / 鼻祖 / 最正宗的"。It's still the OG. 口语/网络语，作文别用。
Keep up [the N] 通用
"继续（保持）…"。Keep up the feedback / Keep up the good work。鼓励别人继续时很顺。

💡 Evening! 是 Good evening 的轻松缩略，做开场问候。paper cuts / performance, reliability, and efficiency（三连排比）是产品圈的常见话术。

Phase 1 · Speaking · FINALE Lesson 0005 · ~10 min

Sentence Stress & Thought Groups （句子重音 + 意群）

The capstone. Everything from Lessons 1–4 lives inside a thought group; pauses live between them. Get this and your English finally flows.

Today's win: Take any sentence, slice it into thought groups, stress the right word in each, and link everything inside — the full system in one move.

The idea （句子不是一条线，是一串意群）

英语句子不是一个词接一个词的平铺直叙，而是一串意群（thought groups）。规则只有两条：

  1. 意群内部：把词连起来（L1–3 连读）、把虚词揉碎成 /ə/（L4）、重读那个关键实词。
  2. 意群之间：停一下（‖）。跨过停顿不要连读——连过去就成了含糊。

① Thought groups — where to pause （在哪停）

A thought group is a few words carrying one idea, said in one breath. Drop a tiny pause (‖) at the end of each. Pause at punctuation, clause boundaries, and meaning breaks — never in the middle of a phrase.

  • I'll be there ‖ in about ten minutes.
  • When I get home, ‖ I'll start dinner.
  • The man in the blue shirt ‖ is my boss.
  • She bought a book, ‖ a pen, ‖ and a notebook.

⚠️ 别在形容词和名词之间停（"a beautiful ‖ car" 是错的）——它们是一个意群。

整句跟读  ·  在 ‖ 处停一下，重读 there / minutes（TTS 不会真停顿真重读，停与重靠你自己控制）

② Sentence stress — which word pops （哪个词重读）

In each group, content words (名词、实义动词、形容词、副词、疑问词) are stressed; function words shrink to schwa (L4). Stress = louder + longer + higher pitch。通常一个意群里只有一个主要重音——承载焦点的那个词。

i WANT tə BUY ə NEW PHONE

主要重音落在 PHONE（新信息/焦点）。其余实词次重，虚词 (i / tə / ə) 几乎消失。

③ Stress shifts meaning （重音改变意思）

同一句话，重读不同的词，意思就变。原理：重读一个词 = 把它当成对比焦点，潜台词是"不是别的，就是这个"。这是经典演示——同一句话能变出 7 种意思：

👇 标记说明：下划线 + 绿色加粗 的词 = 重读（stress），其余正常轻读。

I didn't say he stole the money.
"我没说过他偷了钱。" 潜台词：是别人说的（可能他真偷了，但讲这话的人不是我）。
I didn't say he stole the money.
"我确实没说过他偷了钱。" 潜台词：强调否认（反驳"是你说的"）。
I didn't say he stole the money.
"我没明说他偷了钱。" 潜台词：可能暗示过、提过，但没直接说出口（say 对比 imply）。
I didn't say he stole the money.
"我没说他偷了钱。" 潜台词：钱是被偷了没错，但偷的不是他，是别人。
I didn't say he stole the money.
"我没说他偷了钱。" 潜台词：他拿了那笔钱，但不算"偷"（可能是借的、拿的）。
I didn't say he stole the money.
"我没说他偷了那笔钱。" 潜台词：偷的是另一笔钱，不是"这笔"特定的钱。
I didn't say he stole the money.
"我没说他偷了钱。" 潜台词：他确实偷了东西，但偷的不是钱，是别的（比如首饰）。

所以重音不是装饰——它传递信息焦点。听别人说话时盯重音，就能抓住 ta 真正想强调的；自己说错重音，听众会理解偏。

④ The synthesis （L1–4 总装：一句话拆给你看）

把所有课揉进一句话。方括号 = 意群，‖ = 停顿，大写 = 重读，浅色 = 弱读，· = 连读：

[ i'm gonna PICK·up some·MILK ] ‖ [ on the WAY HOME ]

gonna = going to 揉碎（L4）· PICK·up = 辅音+元音连读（L1）· some·MILK = /m/+/m/ 长读（L2）· on the = 虚词弱读（L4）· ‖ = 意群间停顿（本课）· 大写 = 重读（本课）。

总装整句跟读  ·  把 L1–4 + 本课全部用上：gonna / pick·up / 停顿 / 重读

Why it matters

  • IELTS Speaking grades Fluency & Coherence and Pronunciation — both come down to grouping + stress. Chunk it well and you sound fluent even with grammar mistakes.
  • Being understood: native listeners latch onto the stressed words. Stress the wrong word (or none) and you're hard to follow, however perfect your grammar.
  • The payoff of Phase 1: linking + weak forms + stress + grouping = natural rhythm. That's the whole pronunciation game.

Check yourself （先想，再点开）

1. Where would you pause? "When you arrive call me."
reveal
When you arrive, ‖ call me. — 从句边界后停
2. "I didn't say he stole the MONEY." — what does the stress mean?
reveal
他偷了别的（不是钱）。— money 被重读 = 焦点在"钱"
3. Slice into thought groups: "The book on the table is mine."
reveal
The book on the table ‖ is mine. — 长主语后停一下
Commuter mission: Take one sentence, draw the ‖ where you'd pause, then read it linking inside the groups and pausing between. Do it 3× until the groups feel like single beats.

🎉 Phase 1 complete

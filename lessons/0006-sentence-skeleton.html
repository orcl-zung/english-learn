Phase 2 · Writing Lesson 0006 · ~10 min

Sentence Skeleton （句子骨架：简单 → 并列 → 复合）

Phase 2 begins. Goal: stop translating word-by-word from Chinese and write English that holds together. It all starts here — how a sentence is built.

Today's win: Know the one rule that kills the most common Chinese→English writing error (comma splices), and build all three sentence types on purpose.

The one rule （一条铁律）

中文可以一路逗号连下去，一句话套一堆意思。英语不行。英语的铁律是：

一个英语句子 = 恰好一个主句（一个主语 + 一个谓语动词），
除非你用连接词把更多从句正确地接上。

违反这条，就出现中国学生最常丢分的两个错：逗号拼接和流水句（下面讲）。

The three sentence types （三种句型）

图例：黑色 = 独立主句（能单独成句）· 绿色 = 从句（不能单独成句）· 连接词 = 把从句/主句接起来的词

① Simple 简单句 — 一个主句

She works in London.
The old man in the corner is sleeping. — 主语很长，但依然只有一个主句
Tom and Jerry fight all day. — 复合主语（Tom and Jerry）+ 一个动词 = 仍是简单句

② Compound 并列句 — 两个主句，用 FANBOYS 或分号连接

I was tired, so I went to bed.
She likes tea, but he prefers coffee.
He studied all night; he still failed. — 分号 ; 也能连接两个主句

FANBOYS（七个并列连词）：For · And · Nor · But · Or · Yet · So。两个主句必须靠它们（或分号）连，光逗号不行。

③ Complex 复合句 — 一个主句 + 一个从句，用从属连词连接

Because I was tired, I went to bed.
I went to bed because I was tired.
The book that I bought is excellent. — 定语从句
If it rains, we'll stay home.

常见从属连词：because / since / as（原因）· although / though / while / whereas（转折）· if / unless（条件）· when / before / after / until（时间）· that / which / who（定语从句）。

The two killers （中国学生最常犯的两个错）

❌ Comma splice 逗号拼接

两个完整主句之间只用了一个逗号——这是错的。

✗I love London, it's a great city.

四种改法（任选其一）：

✓I love London because it's a great city. — 变复合句
✓It's a great city, so I love London. — 加 FANBOYS 变并列句
✓I love London; it's a great city. — 用分号
✓I love London. It's a great city. — 直接拆成两句

❌ Run-on 流水句

两个主句之间什么标点都没有。

✗I was tired I went to bed.

❌ Fragment 残句

一个从句被当成完整句子单独出现。

✗Because I was tired. — 从句不能独立成句，得接主句
✓Because I was tired, I went to bed.

Why it matters （雅思写作评分）

  • 雅思写作有 Grammatical Range and Accuracy（语法多样性与准确性）这一评分维度。
  • 5–6 分的写作：要么通篇短简单句（碎），要么满纸 comma splice / run-on（错）—— 这两类直接卡住分数。
  • 7 分+：能自如混用 simple / compound / complex 三种句型，且句子结构不出错。句型多样性本身就会加分。

Check yourself （先判断，再点开）

1. "Although she was busy, she helped me." — simple / compound / complex?
reveal
Complex  — Although 引导从句（绿色那类），加一个主句。
2. "Tom and Jerry fight all day." — 哪种？
reveal
Simple（简单句）  — 陷阱题：复合主语 + 一个动词，仍是一个主句。and 连的是两个名字，不是两个从句。
3. 找错并改正："I was hungry, I ate everything."
reveal
Comma splice（逗号拼接）。改法：I was hungry, so I ate everything. / Because I was hungry, I ate everything. / I was hungry; I ate everything.
4. 合句练习：把 "The test was hard. I passed." 改成 compound 和 complex 各一句。
reveal
Compound: The test was hard, but I passed.
Complex: Although the test was hard, I passed.

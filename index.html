A personal course toward IELTS 7.5+ — built around a 20-minute subway commute. Goal: stop translating word-by-word, and read English aloud so it flows.

Mission: IELTS ≥ 7.5 (Writing ≥ 7.0, Speaking ≥ 7.0). Hook: connected speech first — an audible win that fits the commute and lifts Speaking. Accent: British English.  full mission →

Lesson 0002 · Phase 1 · Speaking
Consonant meets Consonant — hold, merge, drop
Same consonant → hold; /t d/+/j/ → merge; /t d/ between consonants → drop. ~8 min.
Lesson 0003 · Phase 1 · Speaking
Vowel → Vowel Linking — /w/ /j/ /r/
Two vowels collide → a glide slips in: round lip /w/, spread lip /j/, British linking /r/. ~9 min.
Lesson 0004 · Phase 1 · Speaking
The Schwa /ə/ & Weak Forms
Why English sounds fast: grammar words crush into schwa. The #1 fix for the "robotic" Chinese-speaker accent. ~9 min.
Lesson 0005 · Phase 1 · FINALE
Sentence Stress & Thought Groups
The capstone: slice sentences into thought groups, stress the key word, link inside, pause between. ~10 min.
